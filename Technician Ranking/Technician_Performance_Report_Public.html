Quarterly Technician Performance Executive Summary

Methodology: Quality Adjusted Bayesian Ranking

This report ranks technicians based on a Bayesian Weighted Average of their profit per job, penalized by their Recall Rate.

Why this method?

  • Standard averages can be misleading for technicians with low job volume (volatility).
  • This method "shrinks" the average profit of technicians with low volume towards the Company Global Average.
  • New: Scores are now discounted by the technician's Recall Rate. A 10% recall rate reduces the profit score by 10%.

Score Formula: S = [(v / (v + m)) * R + (m / (v + m)) * C] * (1 - RecallRate)

  • v: Technician's job count (Evidence)
  • R: Technician's average profit (Observed Mean)
  • C: Company-wide average profit per job (Prior Mean / Benchmark)
  • m: Smoothing factor (Median job count of the quarter)
  • RecallRate: % of jobs resulting in a repeat call (Penalty)
